Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including post-surgical rehabilitation (TKR, ACL, THR), sports injuries, back and neck pain, arthritis, stroke recovery, pediatric conditions, women's health issues, and general musculoskeletal problems.

Do you provide home physiotherapy services?

Yes! We provide professional home physiotherapy services across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Bangalore, and Tamil Nadu. Our therapists bring portable equipment and provide the same quality care in the comfort of your home.

How long does a typical treatment session last?

A typical physiotherapy session lasts 45-60 minutes. The duration may vary depending on your specific condition and treatment plan. Our therapists will discuss the optimal session length during your initial consultation.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any medical reports, X-rays, MRI scans, or doctor's prescriptions related to your condition. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy movement. Our therapist will conduct a thorough assessment during your first visit.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers. We can provide detailed invoices and treatment documentation that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement. Please contact us to discuss your specific insurance coverage.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your condition, severity, and recovery goals. After the initial assessment, our physiotherapist will create a personalized treatment plan with an estimated number of sessions. Most patients see significant improvement within 6-12 sessions.
